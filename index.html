Our Journey of Compassion 📚

Founded on the principles of empathy and action, Sahajshakti Sarvakalyaan Trust has been a beacon of hope since 2020. We believe every child deserves a chance to thrive, and every community has the potential for positive transformation.

2020: The Spark of Change 📚

Foundation & Vision

Sahajshakti Sarvakalyaan Trust was established with a profound vision: to uplift underprivileged children and communities by addressing critical needs in education and health.

🌟

2022: Expanding Horizons 📚

First Learning Center & Health Camps

We launched our first learning center, educating over 500 children, and expanded health initiatives with medical camps in remote areas.

❤️

: Towards Nationwide Impact 📚

Scaling Programs & Community Empowerment

We aim to reach 10,000 children by scaling our programs, fostering community leadership, and building sustainable livelihoods.

🌟

Our Future: A Shared Dream 📚

Innovation & Collaboration

We plan to innovate with technology, forge partnerships, and advocate for child welfare policies to realize every child's potential.
